Technical Architecture

128-bit Encryption

Every data packet, from researcher identity to raw laboratory findings, is secured using industry-standard AES-128 encryption protocols.

RAG Technology

Our AI search uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation to synthesize accurate answers directly from archived papers.

Supabase Backend

Utilizing high-performance PostgreSQL databases to ensure sub-millisecond query times for global research access.

Atomic Attribution

Every publication is assigned a unique ID, creating an immutable link between the data and the verified author.

The KGR Ethical Charter

  • Verifiability: Research is only archived after verified identity authentication.
  • Transparency: All published data is searchable via the global research feed.
  • Sovereignty: Researchers maintain full control over their drafts until the point of publication.
  • Accessibility: High-integrity research should be available to every lab, regardless of geography.

Global Knowledge Distribution

The KGR Archive is more than a database; it is a global movement to ensure that scientific findings are never lost to institutional silos or paywalls. By decentralizing how research is stored, we create a resilient web of knowledge accessible from any laboratory, anywhere.
