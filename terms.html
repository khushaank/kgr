LEGAL FRAMEWORK

Terms of Research
& Database Usage

Governing the ethical distribution and archival of global scientific findings.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing the KGR Archive, you agree to uphold the integrity of the scientific records contained herein. Users must not attempt to bypass MFA gateways or manipulate research metadata.

2. Researcher Identity

Verified accounts must represent real-world researchers or institutional entities. Impersonation of academic authorities is grounds for immediate Researcher ID revocation.

3. Data Persistence

Once a research paper is moved from 'Draft' to 'Published', it becomes a permanent record of the KGR Archive to ensure long-term citation stability and research auditability.
