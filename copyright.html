LEGAL GOVERNANCE

Intellectual Property &
Copyright Policy

Standardized guidelines for the protection and distribution of scientific truth.

1. Researcher Sovereignty

As established in the KGR Ethical Charter, every researcher maintains full ownership of their original data and findings until the moment of public archival. By publishing to the KGR Archive, authors grant the platform a non-exclusive, global license to host and preserve the record for scientific integrity.

2. Open Access License

Unless otherwise specified, publications in the KGR Archive are distributed under a Sovereign Open Access License. This permits zero-cost public access and academic citation across global research nodes, provided the original author and KGR Archive source are credited.

3. Restrictions & Misuse

  • block Unauthorized commercial redistribution of archived papers is strictly prohibited.
  • block Any alteration of research metadata or author identities constitutes a violation of database integrity.
  • block Scraping for private AI training without institutional API authorization is restricted.

4. Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA)

KGR Archive respects intellectual property rights. If you believe your work has been archived without authorization, please contact the KGR Administrator at khushaankgupta@gmail.com for immediate review and data purge requests.
